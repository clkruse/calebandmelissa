THE INVITATION

Placeholder text and images. We had the pleasure of working with a local letterpress printer to design and create our wedding invitations. The process was a labor of love, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design.

The invitation features a custom illustration of Tower Hill, where our ceremony will take place. We chose a palette of soft blues and greens to reflect the natural beauty of Northern California.

Each invitation was carefully printed on luxurious cotton paper using a vintage letterpress machine. This technique creates a subtle, tactile impression that adds depth and character to the design.

We're excited to share this beautiful piece of craftsmanship with our guests and can't wait to celebrate with everyone on our special day.
